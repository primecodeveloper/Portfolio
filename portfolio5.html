Tattoo Aftercare Tips

Gentle Cleansing

Clean your tattoo with mild, fragrance-free soap and warm water

Protection

Shield your tattoo from direct sunlight during the healing process. 

Avoid Scratching

Refrain from scratching or picking at your tattoo, even if it becomes itchy

Moisturize

Apply a thin layer of cream 2-3 times daily to keep your tattoo hydrated

No Swimming

Avoid swimming in pools until your tattoo is fully healed

Loose Clothing

Wear loose, breathable clothing over your tattoo to minimize friction
